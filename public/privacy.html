Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At segmentor.app, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. We've designed our service from the ground up to respect your privacy by ensuring your data never leaves your device. This policy explains how we achieve complete privacy protection.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or customer data. This includes:

  • No account registration or email addresses
  • No customer data uploads to our servers
  • No analytics or tracking of your usage
  • No cookies for tracking purposes
  • No third-party data sharing

Local Processing Only

All data processing happens entirely in your web browser on your device. When you upload customer data or use segmentor.app:

  • Your data is processed locally using JavaScript in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers or any external services
  • Your data never leaves your computer or device
  • The tool works offline because no server communication is required

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Standard users enjoy completely cookie-free browsing. Brand+ users only require a one-time authentication cookie during initial login, which is immediately moved to local storage—no cookies needed afterward.

Brand+ Authentication

For Brand+ users, authentication is handled through Cloudflare Access. This process:

  • Uses a secure authentication token stored locally (not in cookies)
  • Does not collect or store personal information on our servers
  • Only verifies your access to Brand+ features
  • Does not track your usage or activity

Data You Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can save your work locally using the .seg file format
  • You can export reports and data in various formats (PDF, Excel, PNG)
  • You can delete all data by simply closing your browser or clearing local storage
  • We have no access to your saved files or exported data

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For hosting and Brand+ authentication only. Cloudflare Access handles authentication but does not provide us with your personal data.
  • Font Services: Google Fonts may be loaded from Google's CDN, which is subject to Google's privacy policy.

None of these services have access to your customer data, as all processing happens locally in your browser.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Because we don't collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers, segmentor.app is compliant with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other data protection regulations worldwide

Since we cannot access your data, there's nothing to request, delete, or export—your privacy is protected by design.

Children's Privacy

segmentor.app is not intended for use by children under 13. However, since we don't collect any personal information, we cannot identify or track users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
