# Customisation & Branding FAQ

## What's included in Brand+?
**Keywords:** brand plus, custom branding, logo

Brand+ adds custom branding to your reports:

- Replace the watermark with your company logo
- Apply your brand colours to charts and reports
- Control logo size, position, and orientation

[Learn more about Brand+ →](/brand-plus.html)

## How do I enable Brand+ features?
**Keywords:** enable brand plus, activate branding, upgrade

Brand+ features are available through our upgrade programme. Visit the Brand+ page to learn more about pricing and how to enable custom branding for your reports.

## Can I use my own logo in reports?
**Keywords:** custom logo, upload logo, company logo

Yes, with Brand+. You can upload your company logo via URL or select from predefined partner logos. The logo will replace the default watermark in all exported reports and charts.

## Can I change the colours in my reports?
**Keywords:** brand colours, custom colours, report colours

Yes, with Brand+. You can apply your organisation's colour palette to charts and reports, ensuring brand consistency across all your exported materials.

## What's the difference between free and Brand+?
**Keywords:** free vs brand plus, feature comparison, what's included

All core segmentation and analysis features are free. Brand+ adds visual customisation:

- **Free:** Full access to segmentation, analysis, reports, and exports
- **Brand+:** Everything in free, plus custom logos and brand colours in exports

The functionality is identical—Brand+ is purely about visual branding.
