# Saving & Exporting FAQ

## Can I save my work?
**Keywords:** save work, save project, save analysis

Yes! You can save your complete analysis as a .seg project file that includes:

- All your customer data
- Filter settings and customisations
- Chart configurations and zone sizes
- Manual customer reassignments

Click the save button in the menu to download your project file. You can load it later to continue your analysis exactly where you left off.

## How do I load a previous project?
**Keywords:** load project, open project, continue work

To continue working on a saved project:

1. Click the "Load Project" button in the data entry section
2. Select your .seg file from your computer
3. The tool will restore all your data, settings, and customisations

Your project file contains everything needed to resume your analysis exactly as you left it.

## What export formats are available?
**Keywords:** export reports, download reports, share results, PNG, PDF, CSV, Excel

You can export in multiple formats:

- **PNG:** Chart images for presentations
- **PDF:** Complete reports with charts and analysis
- **CSV:** Raw customer data with segment assignments
- **Excel (XLSX):** Action plans with multiple worksheets

Export options are available in the reporting section and chart controls.

## What types of reports can I generate?
**Keywords:** generate reports, action plan, data report

The tool generates comprehensive reports with multiple sections:

- **Data Report:** Statistics, distributions, and quadrant breakdowns
- **Action Plan:** Strategic recommendations, conversion opportunities, and risk warnings
- **Response Concentration:** Shows where most responses cluster
- **Recommendation Score:** Calculates your overall recommendation score
- **Segment Distribution:** Detailed breakdown by quadrant
- **Proximity Analysis:** Identifies customers close to quadrant boundaries

All reports include visual charts and can be customised before export.

## What advanced analysis features are included?
**Keywords:** proximity analysis, recommendation score, response concentration

The Action Plan includes:

- **Proximity Analysis:** Identifies customers close to quadrant boundaries who could be converted
- **Recommendation Score:** Calculates your overall recommendation score
- **Response Concentration:** Shows where most responses cluster
- **Strategic Priorities:** Highlights high-impact opportunities

## Can I customise reports before exporting?
**Keywords:** customise reports, report settings, report options

Yes. Before exporting, you can customise various aspects of your reports including which sections to include, chart colours, and display preferences. These settings are saved with your project file.

## What's included in the Excel export?
**Keywords:** Excel export, XLSX, spreadsheet export

The Excel export includes multiple worksheets:

- **Raw Data:** All customer data with segment assignments
- **Action sheets:** One sheet per action recommendation with supporting customer lists
- **Opportunity sheets:** One sheet per opportunity with relevant customers
- **Risk sheets:** One sheet per risk warning with affected customers

Each sheet contains customer details including ID, name, email, satisfaction, loyalty, and any additional attributes from your data. Perfect for further analysis in Excel or sharing with team members who prefer spreadsheets.

## Can I export just the chart without the full report?
**Keywords:** export chart, image only, PNG export

Yes. Use the export button in the chart controls to export just the visualisation as a PNG or PDF image. This is perfect for presentations or including in other documents.
