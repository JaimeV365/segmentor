# Understanding the Model FAQ

## What is the Apostles Model?
**Keywords:** what is apostles model, apostles model explanation

The Apostles Model is a customer segmentation framework that classifies customers into four groups based on two dimensions: satisfaction and loyalty. It was developed by Thomas O. Jones and W. Earl Sasser Jr. in their 1995 Harvard Business Review article "Why Satisfied Customers Defect."

Segmentor.app is built on this proven framework‚Äîit's the Apostles Model revamped for modern times with updated terminology and enhanced analytics.

**The original model identified four customer types:**

- **Apostles** (now called Advocates): High satisfaction + High loyalty
- **Mercenaries:** High satisfaction + Low loyalty
- **Hostages:** Low satisfaction + High loyalty
- **Terrorists** (now called Trolls): Low satisfaction + Low loyalty

Segmentor.app brings this powerful framework into the 21st century with modern terminology, interactive visualisation, and advanced analytics.

## How do I interpret Advocates vs Trolls?
**Keywords:** advocates vs trolls, interpret results, understand segments

**Advocates** are your goldmine customers:

- Focus on retaining them and turning them into brand champions
- Ask for referrals and testimonials
- Consider them for beta testing new features

**Trolls** need immediate attention:

- Reach out personally to understand their concerns
- Address their issues quickly to prevent negative word-of-mouth
- Consider if they're worth retaining or if it's time to part ways

## How do I identify Near-Advocates?
**Keywords:** near advocates, identify near advocates, almost advocates

Near-Advocates are customers who are close to becoming full Advocates but need a small push. Look for:

- High satisfaction (4-5) but moderate loyalty (3-4)
- Customers who love your product but haven't fully committed
- Recent customers with high satisfaction scores

Enable the "Show Near-Advocates" option in the tool to highlight these customers. Focus on converting them to full Advocates through loyalty programmes, exclusive offers, or personalised engagement.

## What's the difference between Loyalists and Advocates?
**Keywords:** loyalists vs advocates, difference, superfans

Loyalists are the full quadrant (top-right) of customers with both high satisfaction and high loyalty.

Advocates are the most loyal and satisfied customers within that quadrant‚Äîessentially 'Loyalists Plus.' They don't just stay‚Äîthey actively promote you.

**Think of it like this:** All Advocates are Loyalists, but not all Loyalists are Advocates. Advocates are your superfans.
